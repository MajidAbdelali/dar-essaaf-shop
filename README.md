# dar-essaaf-shop
Dar-Essaaf (دار السعف) is a premium digital boutique dedicated to the soul of Djerbian craftsmanship. Dressed in the iconic Blue and White of the island, we bring the rustic warmth of handwoven palm fronds (Sâf) into the modern home.

From traditional Mdhilla hats to organic home decor, every piece is a tribute to Tunisian heritage - hand-plaited by local artisans to turn ancient textures into timeless design.

"Where the palm meets the sea. Authentic. Rustic. Djerbian."
