The Process

From Palm to Your Home

01

Harvesting

Young palm fronds are hand-harvested from date palm trees during specific seasons, carefully chosen so the tree continues to thrive.

?
02

Drying & Dyeing

Fronds are sun-dried for several weeks, then dyed using traditional natural pigments derived from plants and minerals found on the island.

?
03

Weaving

Artisans weave the dried fronds by hand using patterns passed down over generations. A single basket can take 2 to 3 days to complete.

?
04

Quality Check

Every piece is inspected by our cooperative before packaging. Imperfect pieces are returned to the artisan - no shortcuts, ever.

Planet & People

Sustainable by Nature

Palm weaving uses no electricity, no industrial machinery, and no synthetic chemicals. The raw material - the palm frond - is a natural byproduct of date palm cultivation, a crop central to Djerbian farming for millennia.

  • ? Zero industrial carbon footprint in production
  • ? 100% biodegradable materials
  • ? Artisans earn 3× the regional average wage
  • ? Cooperative model - no middlemen
